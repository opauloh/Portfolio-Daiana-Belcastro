About me

About me

Hello stranger

Nice to meet you

I'm Daiana, a italo-argentinian senior product Designer with 7 years of experience. I've had the privilege of working on projects for prominent companies like Burger King, Ambev, Dell Technologies, INEP, and several other industry leaders. The Nielsen Norman Group has been a significant influence on my professional journey. I'm a devoted follower of their courses, having completed six of them. Each course has been invaluable, contributing immensely to my career development.

Currently, I'm leading innovation as a Product Design Lead at Dell Technologies, focusing on the Dell Design System. Together with my team of four designers, we're dedicated to refining the user experience of the Dell Design System website. Each day is packed with diverse tasks. I guide my team, foster their development, and dive into user research methodologies to ensure our designs truly connect with our audience.

For me, design isn't just a job; it's a passion. I strive to create experiences that not only look great but also deeply resonate with users. With my background, experience, and unwavering commitment, I'm determined to push the boundaries of product design and make a real difference at Dell Technologies.


A glimpse into my personal life

I'm fluent in four languages: Spanish, English, Italian, and Portuguese. Currently, I'm trying to pick up some Japanese. My passion for languages reflects my broader love for different cultures. Having lived in Buenos Aires, Milan, and Belo Horizonte, I've experienced significant personal growth through exposure to diverse environments.

Traveling and hiking are two of my greatest joys. Though I once traveled primarily for leisure, attending weddings has become something usual in recent years. Over the past two years, I've celebrated marriages in Brazil, Argentina, Thailand, California, Tunisia, with Italy slated for next year. These experiences enrich my life, allowing me to immerse myself in various cultures and create lasting memories.
